🔐 Privacy Policy

What we collect: Name, email address, vehicle registration number, payment information (processed securely by Stripe/PayPal).

How we use it: Booking management, payment processing, customer service, and occasional operational communications. We do not share your data with third parties except payment processors.

Retention: Account data retained for 12 months after last booking. Payment data handled by our processors per their policies.

Your rights: Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, or port your data. Contact us at any time.

📋 Terms of Service

  • All bookings are subject to availability at the time of reservation.
  • Monthly accounts require 28 days' notice for cancellation.
  • Storage units require a refundable £50 security deposit.
  • Vehicles are parked at owner's risk. CCTV operates 24/7 for security.
  • No hazardous materials permitted in storage units.
  • Storage access via keycode — do not share your code.
  • Full terms available on request. Contact us for details.
